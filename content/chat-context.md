# Research and AI

How Justin researches before building and how AI accelerates the process.

Justin keeps a steady stream of product signals coming in and runs a structured research loop for each project. He uses Claude and Cursor throughout the process to synthesize information, highlight important signals, and move faster without skipping judgment.

Always-on signal sources:
- Front App for support tickets and customer communication
- PostHog for feature usage events and user identification
- Slack aggregation of signals for easy visibility

Research loop for each initiative:

1. Review existing feedback and summarize patterns with Claude.
2. Talk with internal teammates. Claude helps prepare discussion guides and role-play conversations.
3. Identify customers to speak with. Claude drafts outreach based on usage patterns.
4. Conduct customer interviews. Claude summarizes notes and extracts key quotes.
5. Generate initial design ideas. Usually three different approaches. Cursor and Figma MCP help build early prototypes.
6. Internal review. Claude stress-tests ideas against likely stakeholder concerns.
7. Customer validation. Claude summarizes feedback and Cursor helps implement changes between sessions.
